The Flower Tree’s Mind is a tree-like interactive sculpture. The sculpture functions using Makey Makey and Processing. The sculpture itself represents a simple tree, and the computer, through Processing, is used to demonstrate the mind space of a tree. The project shows how interactions between trees and other living beings may have impact on a tree’s life.

When someone touches any one of the 5 flowers on top of the tree, an image is drawn in the tree’s mind; however, the images will slowly fade away after the tree does not feel anyone’s touch anymore. Without anyone’s touch, the tree’s mind will remain empty until someone interacts with it.
