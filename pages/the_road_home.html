The Road Home is a level I designed using Unreal Engine 4. The level is designed around simple platformer mechanics, such as running and jumping. The goal is to reach your home which is located at the end of the level. The level has jump pads that help you get to higher places if found, checkpoints which you can go off the main course to activate so you don’t have to start from the beginning if you fall off the level (kind of like a side objective), and 2 distinctive parts of the level to visually show the increase of difficulty. The first part of the level is the exterior area with no moving platforms, and the second part has more interior areas with moving platforms. I used purple lighting as a guiding light to show where to go as well as short cuts, and blue lights to show directions toward checkpoints.

In short, this is what going through the level is like. When you start the level all you see is what you see in the image displayed above. You have to get home, but what is home exactly? Well, you probably think it’s that big green planet at the end of the road, right? So you work your way to the planet, but once you touch the planet, it explodes, revealing a whole second area of the level which you now have to explore. The only thing new now is that the platforms here are now moving and that there’s a big building with 2 towers in front of you. So you travel through that building, reach this orb that teleports you to this other dark wrecked building, and find a spaceship at the end of the level. You jump on the ship and it takes off on its own. On the ship you see a purple planet in the distance which the ship is moving towards. Once the ship enters the atmosphere you start to see this ordinary house. Then you realize you’re HOME! You jump off the ship and you see purple lighting around the front door, so you move toward it. Once you reach the front door, a big YOU WIN pops up.
