Dino Arduino is an interactive wearable project of a nervous dinosaur that rests on your shoulders.

Using Arduino, a light sensor and a vibration motor, I made Dino to show off the emotion of fear.

His body is always shaking to show how nervous he is, but the vibration motor increases its speed when someone goes to pet his nose or when the lights go out.

He is scared of strangers and the dark.

When Dino is at his maximum level of fear, the 6 LEDs on his back will light up.
