Uncageable hope is a non-interactive light sculpture, and it is made using Arduino, 1 LED and 1 Tie light. The character inside the cage is named Hugore. He is an imprisoned man who only has his lighter (the Tie light) to escape his cage as his cage bars cannot kept in his light. This is his only form of reaching the outside world.

This sculpture is meant to represent themes of isolation and solitude. Pieces of notes by Hugore are placed all over his cage, which is meant to build on the viewerâ€™s imagination on what Hugore is thinking about and also why he is there.
