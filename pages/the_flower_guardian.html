The Flower Guardian is an interactive sculpture made using Arduino, light sensor and 2 RGBs. The Guardian watches over this flower calmly, but when someone’s hand hovers over it, the Guardian get nervous; when someone is touching the flower, the Guardian gets mad! The Guardian’s eyes change color to represent what emotion he’s going though.

Blue is when nothing is touching the flower, which shows he is calm.

Purple is when something is hovering over the flower, which shows that he is nervous.

Red is when something is touching the flower, which reveals that he is mad!
